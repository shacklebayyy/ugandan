About Ugandan Fixer

Reliable Production Partnership On The Ground

Lead fixer Bill Koske (Bildad Koske) is an industry professional who understands the unique needs of international crews and holds an intimate knowledge of the geography and culture of Uganda and East Africa.

Bill speaks five languages (English, Swahili, Luganda, and some French) and has the unique capability to bring together the best local production resources to ensure your project's success.

Local Connections

Strong ties with local agencies, communities, and national park management for seamless access.

Media Accreditation
